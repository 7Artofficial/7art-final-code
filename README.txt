How to deploy on Netlify:
1. Extract this ZIP.
2. Upload the complete extracted folder to Netlify Deploys.
3. Do not upload only index.html.
4. After deploy, test:
   - Home page
   - Leadership page
   - Contact form
   - Thank-you page
   - WhatsApp and Email floating buttons

Form submissions:
Netlify Dashboard > Your Site > Forms > contact > Submissions

Email notification:
Netlify Dashboard > Your Site > Forms > contact > Form notifications > Add email notification
